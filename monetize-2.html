DATA PRIVACY AND SECURITY

Transparency: Organizations should be transparent about their data practices, including their privacy policies and how data is used.
Data Protection: Implement security measures to prevent unauthorized access, breaches, and data leaks.
Legal Compliance: Comply with relevant data protection laws and regulations, such as GDPR in the European Union and CCPA in California.
Access Control: Implement strong user authentication and authorization mechanisms to ensure that only authorized individuals can access data.
