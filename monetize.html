DATA SOURCES

Telematics: Data on vehicle performance, location, speed, and diagnostics.
Sensors: Information from various sensors, such as accelerometers, gyroscopes, and proximity sensors.
Cameras: Visual data from onboard cameras, often used for advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS).
Infotainment Systems: Data from entertainment systems, including user preferences and usage patterns.
Connectivity: Data from connected services, such as navigation, real-time traffic, and remote vehicle control.
